Approved by AICTE

The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) is a national-level body that governs technical education in India. Purpose: AICTE's purpose is to develop and plan technical education in India, ensuring that the education system is coordinated and integrated.

Accredited by NBA

The National Board of Accreditation (NBA) is an independent body that assesses the quality of programs offered by technical institutions in India. Objectives: Ensuring the quality and relevance of technical education, assessing the outcomes of programs, and helping graduates meet industry requirements.

Accredited by NAAC with A Grade

The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) is an autonomous institution of the University Grants Commission (UGC) that assesses and accredits colleges and universities in India. Mission: The NAAC's mission is to ensure that quality assurance is an integral part of higher education.
